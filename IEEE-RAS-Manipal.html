Welcome to 
IEEE Robotics & Automation Society
Manipal Chapter

At the Manipal Chapter of IEEE Robotics & Automation Society, we strive to push the global IEEE RAS agenda: foster the development and facilitate the exchange of scientific and technological knowledge in Robotics and Automation domains of engineering to benefit our constituent members, the profession, and humanity.

We aim to do so through activites conducted for constituent members along with seminars and workshops for members and other students alike. Inducted committee members will be given the opportunity to also be part of the behind the scenes working of the organization. They can be part of any two of five subsystems of the organization
